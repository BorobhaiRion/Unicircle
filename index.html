About Unicircle

The UniCircle Kickstart Dashboard is a centralized platform designed to help university students manage their everyday non-academic needs in one secure place. Instead of juggling scattered sources like social media or generic marketplaces, students can use this dashboard to buy and sell used items, find shared housing, or connect with tutors and learners—all within a trusted student-only environment. With an intuitive interface and built-in verification, UniCircle ensures safety, convenience, and collaboration, allowing students to focus more on their academic goals while staying supported by their peers.

Key Features

User Profile & Verification

Secure registration with university email verification and a profile snapshot with edit and update options.

Marketplace Management

Post and browse used items with descriptions, images, and prices. Track active, sold, and pending listings.

Housing Hub

Post accommodation ads and browse listings with filters. Connect safely with verified roommates or landlords.

Tutoring & Learning

Tutors can create profiles and learners can post “Tutor Needed” requests. Search and filter tutors by subject, rating, or schedule.

Communication Center

Built-in chat for buyers, sellers, roommates, and tutors. Notifications for new messages and requests.

Trust & Transparency

Rating and review system for all interactions. Track feedback on transactions, housing, and tutoring sessions.
